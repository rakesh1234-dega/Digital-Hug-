Meditation Tips

  • Find a quiet and comfortable space.
  • Start with short sessions (5-10 minutes) and gradually increase the duration.
  • Don't worry about having a "perfect" meditation; just focus on the present moment.
  • Use guided meditations if you're new to meditation.
  • Practice regularly for best results.
  • Be patient and kind to yourself.
